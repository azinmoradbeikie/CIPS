What CIPS do?

CIPS provide accurate identification, localization, and tracking of assets and people in indoor environments, leveraging a variety of localization technologies, techniques, and algorithms to meet specific requirements and limitations.
Why CIPS?

The primary goal of CIPS is to develop a cost-effective and efficient solution for accurately localizing and tracking assets in indoor and outdoor environments, based on their required level of accuracy.
